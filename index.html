Min-Sung Yoon (Mason)

Ph.D. Candidate at KAIST

I am a Ph.D. student in the School of Computing at KAIST, advised by Prof. Sung-Eui Yoon. I am a member of the SGVR Lab and have had the privilege of collaborating with Prof. Daehyung Park.

I am primarily interested in reinforcement learning (RL) for embodied AI and robotics foundational models (RFM). I actively explore new domains that bridge diverse research areas, including computer vision and computer graphics. I believe that, ultimately, many research directions converge toward robotics with the shared goal of enhancing human life.
