I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science (CS) at Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST), where I am advised by Prof. Sung-Eui Yoon. I am currently a member of the Scalable Graphics, Vision, and Robotics (SGVR) Lab and have the privilege of collaborating closely with Prof. Daehyung Park at KAIST.

• Research interest: I am particularly interested in increasing the autonomy of robots in various ways: 🧑‍💻➡🤖➕🧠. My research interest spans the following keywords: Reinforcement learning, Embodied AI, 3D Vision, Deep learning, Robotics, Motion Generation/Planning, Task and Motion Planning, Agile and Robust Control, Navigation, Graphics, etc. While my interests may appear broad, I am continuously drawn to exploring new research domains and connecting disparate fields. I hold a belief that all research fields eventually converge into robotics with the goal of enhancing quality of human-being life.
